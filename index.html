Our Mission

To solve the San Francisco housing crisis, our utmost priority is to bring you the true, unbiased facts concerning this monumental issue, and dedicate our time to creating applicable solutions that will bring this dilemna to an end. With your help and support, we will be able to put this crisis to an end once and for all.

The Problem

San Francisco has a problem. It has some of the highest rents in the country; $3.76 per square foot, on average. People are being evicted, only half of needed housing units are being constructed each year, and it can take decades to get a structure built. To learn more about how this could have occurred, in the first place visit our 'Problem' page.

Our Solution

To fix this multifaceted crisis, we plan to: overhaul the planning process used in the construction of new housing units, attain more land (for housing), develop SRO housing, create a public relations campaign, preserve fair rent control, build more transporation systems, and increase taxes on tech companies. These changes are long overdue, and you can learn more about their specifics below.

How You Can Help

First and foremost, you can help support our efforts through your vote, as this would lead to the implementation of life-changing policies that will ultimately end the housing crisis in San Francisco, but you can also help make a difference by learning more about the crisis and spreading awareness of the issue to help the people currently in power make the best decisions as possible.
